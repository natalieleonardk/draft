K-State Enrollment Statistics Over The Past 10 Years

Natalie Leonard | For MC 316

November 21, 2020

The fall of 2010 saw K-State instructing 23, 588 students. Today, nearly 11 years later, K-State is like universities around the world, struggling to maintain enrollment high numbers during the COVID-19 pandemic. The university is teaching nearly three thousand less students with enrollment sitting at 20, 854 this semester. After analyzing the enrollment data from years past, interviewing K-State’s enrollment professionals and speaking with students who returned to K-State this semester and others who chose to take time off, it is clear there are many factors at play when students decide where they’d like to get their college education.
